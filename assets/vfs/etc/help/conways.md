## What it is

## Controls

- Arrow keys: move cursor
- `Space` / `Enter`: toggle cell
- `p`: run/pause
- `n`: single step
- `r`: random fill
- `c`: clear
- `+` / `-`: speed
- `s`: save
- `Esc` / `q`: quit
